About Monster Survivors

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game that puts your skills to the ultimate test. As waves of monsters descend upon you, you must strategically upgrade your abilities, collect resources, and fight for survival.

Key Features

  • Character progression system
  • Multiple weapon types
  • Strategic upgrade paths
